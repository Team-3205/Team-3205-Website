ABOUT TEAM 3205

Founded in 2010, FRC Team 3205 Patriots is a student-run FIRST Robotics team at Concord Carlisle High School in Massachusetts that competes in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). The CCHS Robotics club is a team that participates in the FRC Robotics league. During the competition season, we operate much like a sports team: for six weeks starting in early January, we meet daily to design, build, and test a robot to compete in league meets. In March, we participate in two separate competitions, each held over a period of three days. During the off-season, the club meets twice per week.

FIRST ROBOTICS COMPETITION

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an international high school robotics competition. Each year, teams of high school students and mentors work during a six-week period to build game-playing robots that weigh up to 125 pounds. Robots complete tasks such as scoring balls into goals, flying discs into goals, inner tubes onto racks, hanging on bars, and balancing robots on balance beams. The game changes yearly, keeping the excitement fresh and giving each team a more level playing field. Last year's competition was titled "FIRST Rapid React"

SPECIFICATIONS

DRIVETRAIN

Mecanum drive powered by four Falcon 500 motors with field-oriented driving.

TWO JOINTED ARM

Powered by two Falcon motors, can place low, medium, and high on the grid.

CLAW

Powered by two 4-inch pneumatics, can hold both cones and cubes. IR sensor automatically closes when a cone or cube is near.

AUTONOMOUS

Reliable cone high and taxi. Experimental cube high, cone high, and taxi. Experimental cone high and auto balance.

PERFORMANCE

NE District - North Shore Event - Reading 2023

Team 3205 was Rank #19 with a record of 6-6-0 We were chosen as a backup team for the playoffs but unfortunately did not get to step in.

NE District - WPI Event - WPI 2023

Team 3205 was rank #8 with a record of 11-6-0 We were the alliance captains for the 5th seeded alliance alongside teams 4905 and 348. We progressed well through the playoffs, upset the number one seeded alliance in the semi-finals, and went on to win 2nd place making us district event finalists!

New England FIRST District Championship - Springfield 2023

Team 3205 was rank #7 in the MEIR Division with a record of 11-4-0 We were the alliance captains for the 5th-seeded alliance alongside teams 6324 and 5813. We progressed well through the playoffs, but unfortunately were eliminated in the semi-finals. With our slow start to the season, we ended with our team being ranked 38th overall accumulated, not quite enough ranking points to attend the FIRST World Championships. Though we were unable to attend the championship we ended the 2023 season strong with a record of 28-16-0 and a total ranking point count of 149.
